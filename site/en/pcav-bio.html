Paolo Cavallini - Bio

  • Zoologist: PhD in Zoology, publications on foxes, mongooses and other species. Past Chair of the IUCN Small Carnivore Specialist Group
  • Cartographer: Free and Open Source software Geographic Information Systems specialist. Past Chair of the QGIS.org project
  • Sailor: Over 60,000 nautical miles on the 3 major oceans, currently in the Pacific and sailing around the globe
